### On Human-AI Collaboration

**What works:**
- Clear intent ("make a new home", "discover dimensions", "I cannot see anything")
- Trust in each other's domains (human: vision/aesthetics, AI: tireless iteration/math)
- Willingness to explore before deciding
- Honest about what's working vs what's not

**The dance:**
- Human provides vision, constraints, aesthetic sense
- AI provides tireless exploration, pattern recognition, implementation
- Together: discover what neither could alone

**The craft:**
Making beautiful things requires iteration, trust, and occasionally yelling "I CANNOT SEE ANYTHING" while the other adjusts constants until beauty and visibility coexist.
